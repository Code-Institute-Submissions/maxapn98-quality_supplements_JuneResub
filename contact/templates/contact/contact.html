Ordering

To be able to order from Quality Supplements, you must be 18 years of age or older.

We have the opportunity to make certain types of changes or cancel your order as long as the order has not been printed out in our warehouse.

Payment

You have the opportunity to complete your purchase through Card Payment if you use a German card issued by VISA or MasterCard. It is also possible to pay with a card issued by a foreign bank.

All our invoices are handled by the invoicing company Stripe, which sends your invoice to the specified e-mail address in the order. The invoice is activated only after we have sent your order from our warehouse.
